Fried Rice


Description: Fried Rice is the Best food you can have in your life with lot of veggies and meat.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Spring Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Eggs and chicken
  • Oil
  • Garlic

Making Procedure:

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Cut veggies and finely chop garlic and onions
  3. Fry the veggies
  4. Boil and scrammble eggs
  5. Fry chicken
  6. Add fried veggies and chicken to rice
  7. Mix it well and Serve!
